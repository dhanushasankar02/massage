Therapist FAQs

Are all therapists licensed?
Yes, absolutely. Every therapist on the Zenith Wellness platform is fully licensed, insured, and has undergone a rigorous background check.
Can I request the same therapist again?
Of course! If you find a therapist you love, you can easily rebook them through your client dashboard.
Do therapists bring their own equipment?
Yes, our therapists bring everything needed for a perfect session, including the massage table (or chair), fresh linens, lotions, and soothing music.
What is the tipping policy?
Tips are not required but are always appreciated. You can easily add a tip during the booking process or after your session is complete.

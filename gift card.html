Common Questions

No, our gift cards never expire. Your recipient can use them whenever they need a moment of peace.

Yes! The balance can be applied to both the cost of the session and any gratuity they wish to add for the therapist.
Can I schedule the email delivery?
Absolutely. During checkout, you can pick a specific date for the gift card email to arrive in their inbox, making it perfect for birthdays.

Currently, we only offer digital e-gift cards to ensure immediate delivery and zero environmental waste.
